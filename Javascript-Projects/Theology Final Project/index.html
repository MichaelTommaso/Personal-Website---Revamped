What is the Goal of This Website?

Hello, I am Michael Tommaso, a student at Regis High School, commmited to doing justice in my community. This website that I have designed is designated to spreading awareness to the difficulty and hypocrisy that an ex-convict experiences, while entering back into society. I hope that with this website, I will be able to highlight the most critical aspects of life that are hindered by stigmas surrounding ex-convcits. These walls hurt our entire community, and by spreading awareness, we can make ex-convicts no longer defined by their past mistakes, but rather productive members of society looking towards the future.

Helpful Resources to Checkout for Ex-Offenders.

Jails to Jobs

Jails to Jobs, a Section 501(c)(3) nonprofit, is an organization that gives ex-offenders the tools they need to find employment.

CEO

Center for Employment Opportunities offers individuals just coming home from prison the ongoing support necessary to build career capital and financial stability.

FICGN

Formerly Incarcerated College Graduates Network is a network of college graduates who have been to prison.

NELP

National Employment Law Project fights for policies to create good jobs, expand access to work, and strengthen protections and support for low-wage workers and unemployed workers.

Comunity Change

Comunity Change is an organization that specializes in Social Justice for all. The specfic section I have linked is about the stigmas that ex-convicts face socialy and economically.
