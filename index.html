Websites I Designed for School

This website that I have designed is designated to spreading awareness to the difficulty and hypocrisy that an ex-convict experiences, while entering back into society. By posting it directly onto my school's website, I spread awareness to this topic that I deam very important. I also made QR codes and posted them everywhere around my school for people looking from the outside in to understand the injustices. Lastly, I uploaded this website into multiple facebook groups and helped ex-convicts access the job market and find jobs best set for them.

You can find the source code and Python files on my Github Page found in the Contacts section.
